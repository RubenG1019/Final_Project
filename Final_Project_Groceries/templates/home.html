About

Simply a page that helps people avoid the stress of having to go all the way to various stores in order to get their desired products when they could easily purchase them online!!!

Documentation

Product Usage

Anyone who needs to buy or sell grocries. This is an easier and conducive way of trading without having to go all the way to the store. This enables users to both purchase and sell groceries.

Aspects of Functionality

A user has the option to register and become a buyer or register and be a seller. Regitration is done by inputing your full names, address and password.
Once the information has been entered, the user will be taken to a page with that various stores and can add anything they wish to purchase in the cart. They can also register products that they have to sell for other users to purchase
