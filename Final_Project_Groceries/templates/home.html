About

Bringing awareness to people about weather conditions by providing them with information about how the weather will be on the current day and in three days time. This weather app can help users make decisions about their attire and daily activities.

Here are some examples of places that are experiencing terrible weather conditions in the world. click here! We are here to alert them of the current weather, so they could adequately prepare for what is to come!

Documentation

Product Usage

Anyone who needs to know the weather information can benefit from this website to gain better insight on what to wear for that day as well as 3 days in advance. This will also help with planning activities affected by the weather.

Aspects of Functionality

A user has the option to either input a city name, log in, or register with ther email and password.
Once the information has been entered, the user will be taken to a page with that locations weather information as well as climate change information based on their location as well
